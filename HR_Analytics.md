## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing employee data to identify attrition trends and key workforce insights that support HR decision-making.

## Dataset
- Employee demographic details
- Job roles and departments
- Attrition status
- Performance and tenure data

## Key Insights
- Identified attrition rate across departments and job roles
- Higher attrition observed among employees with lower tenure
- Certain job roles showed consistently higher attrition trends

## Dashboards & KPIs
- Overall attrition rate KPI
- Attrition by department and job role
- Attrition by tenure group
- Employee distribution insights

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization
